How to Play Battleship Online

Battleship is a classic two-player strategy guessing game. Each player secretly arranges their fleet on a 10x10 grid, then takes turns calling out grid coordinates to find and sink the opponent's ships. The first player to sink every enemy ship wins.

Rules

  1. Each player places their fleet on a private 10x10 grid before the game begins.
  2. Players alternate turns, each turn calling out one coordinate (e.g., B4).
  3. A "hit" is announced when a coordinate lands on an opponent's ship; a "miss" when it does not.
  4. A ship is sunk when every cell it occupies has been hit.
  5. The game ends when all ships of one player have been sunk — the other player wins.

Fleet Composition

  • Carrier — 5 cells
  • Battleship — 4 cells
  • Cruiser — 3 cells
  • Submarine — 3 cells
  • Destroyer — 2 cells

AI Difficulty Levels

Easy
The AI fires at random coordinates each turn — great for beginners learning the basics.
Medium
After scoring a hit, the AI hunts adjacent cells to finish off the ship before returning to random fire.
Hard
The AI uses a probability-density algorithm to target the most likely ship positions, making it a formidable opponent.

Real-Time Multiplayer

Challenge a friend online by creating a private room and sharing the room code, or let the matchmaker pair you with a random opponent. Games are played in real time over a WebSocket connection — no refresh needed. No account is required to play, but creating a free account lets you track your wins on the leaderboard.
